It can be hard to fall asleep when you're on fire
For flute and live electronics
2014

World premiere by Willie Santiago at East Carolina University April 2014

Pieces are often constructed through overt choices by one or a small number individuals. This is not the only possible manner in which music can be made. Crickets and other insects create their songs collectively. By playing along with eight virtual instrumentals, the player in this piece can participate in a similar process where each member of the ensemble contributes to the overall texture. Rather than any one agent deciding exactly how the piece should develop, each voice has a roughly equal say in the way the piece unfolds.

Second performance by Laura Pillman at University of North Texas Electric LaTex Festival November 2014
