We are restless things
for four marimbas
2013

Shapes can either be drawn explicitly or they can be implied through a number of smaller strokes. So to are the gestures in the piece. Each player is given a series of patterns to repeat, and a general scheme for how to synchronize with the rest of the ensemble. Three large sweeping gestures emerge from the cloudy textures the players' overlapping parts create.
